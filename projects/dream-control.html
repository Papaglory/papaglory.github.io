Dream Control: A 3-Hour Game Creation Challenge

Welcome to the page for Dream Control, a Unity 3D platformer created during a friendly game creation challenge with my brother where we each made our own game. This project, crafted in three hours, takes you on a dark journey through the depths of the subconscious mind. Navigate dream levels and cutscenes as you attempt to save yourself.

For an in-depth look at the codebase for this project, please refer to the Github repository.

Project Overview

Dream Control features cutscenes and simple 3D platforming mechanics in the form of snowboarding and driving.

  • Cutscenes: Explore dreamscapes and cutscenes representing the subconscious mind.
  • Gameplay: Drive through levels, collect coins, and progress through various stages.
